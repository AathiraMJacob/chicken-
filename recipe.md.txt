1 wash the chicken properly 
2 put all the masalas and the curd on the chicken and mix it properly
3 keep it in the fridge for 4 to 6 hours
4 take it out 
5 put it in a small pan 
6 put some water and heat it till all the water evaporates
7 your diet chicken is ready 
p.s. if you want it to be tatier fry it instead of boiling it as instructed here
